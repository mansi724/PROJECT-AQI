# DPCC — Ban on Manufacture, Sale and Use of Firecrackers

The Delhi Pollution Control Committee (DPCC), ahead of each winter/Diwali season,
notifies a complete ban on the production, storage, sale and use of all types of
firecrackers (including so-called "green" crackers) in the NCT of Delhi.

## Key points
- The ban typically runs through the winter high-pollution season (covering
  Diwali) and is enforced by Delhi Police and DPCC teams.
- Online sale and delivery of firecrackers into Delhi is also prohibited.
- Firecracker bursting during Diwali causes a sharp overnight spike in PM2.5 and
  metallic aerosols, compounding the winter smog and stubble-transport load.
- Violations attract penalties under the Air (Prevention and Control of
  Pollution) Act and the Explosives Act.

## Relevance
This is the primary regulatory lever for the Diwali firecracker spike, a
short-lived but intense combustion source visible in ward-level AQI around the
festival window.
