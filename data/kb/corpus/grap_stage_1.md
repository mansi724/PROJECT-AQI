# GRAP Stage I — "Poor" (AQI 201–300)

The Graded Response Action Plan (GRAP) is the emergency air-quality response
framework for Delhi-NCR, invoked by the Commission for Air Quality Management
(CAQM). Stage I applies when the AQI is in the **Poor (201–300)** band.

## Core measures under Stage I
- Enforce dust-mitigation norms at all construction and demolition (C&D) sites;
  cover materials, use anti-smog guns on large sites.
- Mechanised / vacuum sweeping of roads and daily water sprinkling on identified
  dust-prone corridors.
- Strict control of dust at C&D sites larger than 500 m²; sites must be
  registered on the dust-monitoring portal.
- Ban on open burning of solid waste and biomass; enforce penalties.
- Ensure proper functioning of pollution-control devices in industries and
  thermal power plants.
- Tighten enforcement of PUC (Pollution Under Control) norms; identify and
  penalise visibly polluting vehicles.
- Restrict use of diesel generator (DG) sets except for essential/emergency use.

## Health guidance
Sensitive groups (children, elderly, people with respiratory or cardiac
conditions) should reduce prolonged or heavy outdoor exertion. General public is
largely unaffected but may notice minor discomfort.
