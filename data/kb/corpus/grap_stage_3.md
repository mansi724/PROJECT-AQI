# GRAP Stage III — "Severe" (AQI 401–450)

Stage III adds stringent restrictions on top of Stage I and II when the AQI
enters the **Severe (401–450)** band.

## Additional measures under Stage III
- Ban on construction and demolition (C&D) activities in Delhi-NCR, except for
  essential projects (railways, metro, airports, national security, healthcare)
  and non-polluting activities such as plumbing, interior and electrical work.
- Ban on operation of stone crushers and all mining / associated activities.
- Restriction on **BS-III petrol and BS-IV diesel** light motor vehicles
  (4-wheelers) in Delhi and neighbouring districts; enforced by traffic police.
- Closure of brick kilns, hot-mix plants and industries not running on approved
  cleaner fuels (unless connected to gas supply).
- State governments may consider restricting classes up to Grade 5 to a hybrid
  (physical + online) mode.
- Intensify all Stage I–II dust, sweeping and public-transport measures.

## Health guidance
Everyone should avoid outdoor physical activity; sensitive groups should remain
indoors and keep activity low. Use air purifiers where available; wear N95 masks
if going outdoors is unavoidable.
