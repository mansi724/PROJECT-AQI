# GRAP Stage II — "Very Poor" (AQI 301–400)

Stage II measures are added on top of all Stage I measures when the AQI enters
the **Very Poor (301–400)** band.

## Additional measures under Stage II
- Intensify mechanised sweeping and daily water sprinkling on roads, especially
  at hotspots and identified dust corridors.
- Increase parking fees to discourage private vehicle use, coupled with augmented
  CNG / electric bus and Metro services (additional fleet, higher frequency).
- Ban the use of coal and firewood, including in tandoors at hotels, restaurants
  and open eateries.
- Restrict / regulate use of diesel generator sets across sectors (with defined
  exemptions for essential services and emergencies).
- Deploy traffic police at identified congestion points to smooth flow and cut
  idling emissions.
- Target enforcement at the 13 identified pollution hotspots in Delhi.

## Health guidance
People with heart or lung disease, older adults and children should avoid
prolonged or heavy outdoor exertion; others should reduce it. Consider N95
masks outdoors and keep windows closed during peak pollution hours.
