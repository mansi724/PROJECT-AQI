# GRAP Stage IV — "Severe+" (AQI > 450)

Stage IV is the most stringent level, adding emergency restrictions on top of
Stages I–III when the AQI exceeds **450**.

## Additional measures under Stage IV
- Ban on entry of truck traffic into Delhi (except trucks carrying essential
  commodities / providing essential services, and all LNG/CNG/electric trucks).
- Ban on Delhi-registered diesel-run medium and heavy goods vehicles, except
  those carrying essential commodities.
- Ban on all construction and demolition activities, including essential public
  projects such as highways, flyovers, pipelines and power transmission.
- Restrictions extended to BS-III/BS-IV four-wheelers as in Stage III, with
  tighter enforcement.
- State governments to consider: closing schools/colleges, non-emergency
  commercial activity, and allowing **50% of staff to work from home**.
- Consider additional emergency measures: odd-even vehicle rationing, halting
  non-essential offices.

## Health guidance
Public-health emergency for air quality. Everyone should stay indoors as far as
possible, run purifiers, and strictly avoid outdoor exertion. Sensitive groups
are at serious risk.
